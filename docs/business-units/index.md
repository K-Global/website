# Nine units, one system

K Global covers the whole map through **nine business units**. They aren't
separate airlines — they share one identity and one crew room — they're
distinct *ways to fly*, each with its own mandate, fleet and product. Pick the
one that matches how you like to fly, or fly them all.

| Unit | Mandate | Haul | Typical fleet | Product |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **K Premier** | Flagship premium on marquee routes | Ultra-long | A350-1000 · 777-300ER · A380 · 747-8i | 4-cabin, incl. First |
| **K Intercontinental** | Everyday long-haul mainline | Long | A350-900 · 787-9 · 777 · A330 | 3-cabin |
| **K Connect** | Full-service short & medium-haul, hub to city | Short/medium | A321 · A320 · A220 · 737 · E-Jets | 2-cabin |
| **K Express** | Low-cost, point-to-point | Short/medium | A320/A321neo · 737-800 hi-density | Single-cabin LCC |
| **K Regional** | Thin-market feed into the hubs | Regional | E195-E2 · CRJ · ATR · Dash 8 | Feed |
| **K Charter** | Group & ad-hoc charter | Short–long | A320 family · A330 · 767 | Charter |
| **K Holidays** | Leisure flights to holiday destinations | Short–long | A321 · A330 · A350 · 787 | Leisure |
| **K Academy** | Training & new-pilot progression | Short/regional | Trainers · regional & narrowbody types | Training |
| **K Cargo** | Main-deck freight | Long | 777F · 747-8F · MD-11F | Freight |

## Find your kind of flying

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-star-circle:{ .lg .middle } __Premium & long-haul__

    ---

    **K Premier** and **K Intercontinental** fly the world's marquee routes in
    wide-body comfort — the flagship experience, First cabin included.

-   :material-city:{ .lg .middle } __Short-haul & low-cost__

    ---

    **K Connect** and **K Express** keep the network breathing — full-service
    hub-to-city and dense low-cost point-to-point.

-   :material-beach:{ .lg .middle } __Leisure, charter & feed__

    ---

    **K Holidays** and **K Charter** fly people to the sun; **K Regional**
    turboprops and jets feed the hubs.

-   :material-school:{ .lg .middle } __Learn & fly freight__

    ---

    **K Academy** brings new pilots up through the ratings; **K Cargo** runs
    main-deck freighters across the long-haul network, day and night.

</div>

!!! note "One identity, nine missions"
    Every unit shares the K Global brand, the paddler symbol and one crew room.
    What sets them apart is the mission — who they carry, how far, and in what.
    Detailed per-unit pages are on the way; for now this table is the map.
