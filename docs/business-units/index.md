# Nine business units, one callsign

K Global covers the whole market through **nine business units**. They aren't
separate brands — on frequency it's all **KAY ("Kayak")** — they're distinct
*kinds of flying*, each with its own mandate, fleet and product. Pick the one
that matches how you like to fly, or fly them all.

| Unit | Mandate | Haul | Typical fleet | Product |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Premier** | Flagship premium on marquee routes | Ultra-long | A350-1000 · 777-300ER · A380 · 747-8i · Concorde | 4-cabin, incl. First |
| **Intercontinental** | Standard long-haul mainline | Long | A350-900 · 787-9 · 777 · A330 | 3-cabin |
| **Regular** | Capitals & key cities, full-service | Short/medium | A320/A321 · 737 · A220 | 2-cabin |
| **City** | Secondary cities, full-service | Short | A220 · A319 · E-Jets | 2-cabin |
| **Express** | Low-cost, point-to-point | Short/medium | A320/A321neo · 737-800 hi-density | Single-cabin LCC |
| **Regional** | Thin-market feed | Regional | E195-E2 · CRJ · ATR · Dash 8 | Feed |
| **Charter / Vacation** | Leisure & holiday | Short–long | A320 family · A330/A350 · 767 | Leisure |
| **Executive** | Business jets / VIP | Any | Gulfstream · Falcon · Challenger · ACJ/BBJ | Private |
| **Cargo** | Main-deck freight | Long | 777F · 747-8F · MD-11F | Freight |

## Find your kind of flying

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-star-circle:{ .lg .middle } __Premium & long-haul__

    ---

    **Premier** and **Intercontinental** fly the world's marquee routes in
    wide-body comfort — the flagship experience, First cabin included.

-   :material-city:{ .lg .middle } __Short-haul & low-cost__

    ---

    **Regular**, **City** and **Express** keep the network breathing —
    full-service mainline, secondary cities, and dense low-cost point-to-point.

-   :material-airplane-takeoff:{ .lg .middle } __Feed & leisure__

    ---

    **Regional** turboprops and jets feed the hubs; **Charter / Vacation**
    flies holidaymakers to the sun.

-   :material-package-variant-closed:{ .lg .middle } __Executive & cargo__

    ---

    **Executive** flies business jets and VIP charter; **Cargo** runs
    main-deck freighters across the long-haul network, day and night.

</div>

!!! note "Distinguished by market, not by brand"
    Every unit shares one identity, one callsign and one `D-AK…` fleet. What
    sets them apart is the mission — who they carry, how far, and in what.
    Detailed per-unit pages are on the way; for now this table is the map.
