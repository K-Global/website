# Around 421 aircraft, Concorde to turboprop

K Global flies a realistic, **Lufthansa-Group-shaped fleet of roughly 421
aircraft**, wearing a single German-scheme `D-AK…` identity. It spans the full
range of civil aviation — from a supersonic flagship to short-field
turboprops, from VIP business jets to main-deck freighters. Whatever rating
you enjoy, there's a type here for it.

## Freighters

The **Cargo** unit runs a main-deck freighter fleet — **777F, 747-8F and
MD-11F** — flying the long-haul network around the clock. If you like flying
heavy metal into quiet night airports, this is your corner of K Global.

!!! tip "A fleet for every rating"
    Because K Global models the whole group, you're never boxed into one type.
    Start on a narrow-body out of Frankfurt and work up to the flagship
    wide-bodies — or specialise in turboprops, business jets or freight.
    [See how to join.](../join.md)
