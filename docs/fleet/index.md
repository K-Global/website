# Around 421 aircraft, flagship to turboprop

K Global flies a realistic fleet of **roughly 421 aircraft** spanning the full
range of civil aviation — from flagship wide-bodies to short-field turboprops,
from leisure jets to main-deck freighters. Whatever rating you enjoy, there's a
type here for it, and a business unit that flies it.

## By category

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-airplane:{ .lg .middle } __Flagship wide-body__

    ---

    **A380 · A350-1000 · 747-8i · 777-300ER** — the four-cabin heavy metal on
    **K Premier**'s marquee routes.

-   :material-airplane:{ .lg .middle } __Long-haul wide-body__

    ---

    **A350-900 · 787-9 · A340-600 · A330-900** — the intercontinental
    workhorses of **K Intercontinental** and **K Holidays**.

-   :material-airplane:{ .lg .middle } __Narrow-body__

    ---

    **A321 · A320 · A319 · A220 · 737** — full-service short-haul and dense
    low-cost, across **K Connect** and **K Express**.

-   :material-airplane:{ .lg .middle } __Regional jets & turboprops__

    ---

    **E-Jets · CRJ · ATR · Dash 8 · Saab 340** — the **K Regional** feed fleet
    into the thin markets.

-   :material-airplane:{ .lg .middle } __Leisure & charter__

    ---

    **A321 · A330 · A350 · 767** — sun-bound metal for **K Holidays** and
    **K Charter**.

-   :material-airplane:{ .lg .middle } __Freighters__

    ---

    **777F · 747-8F · MD-11F** — **K Cargo**'s main-deck fleet, flying the
    long-haul network around the clock.

</div>

!!! tip "A fleet for every rating"
    Because K Global models the whole map, you're never boxed into one type.
    Start on a narrow-body out of Frankfurt with **K Academy** and work up to
    the flagship wide-bodies — or specialise in turboprops, leisure or freight.
    [See how to join.](../join.md)
