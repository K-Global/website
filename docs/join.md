# Join the crew

Ready to fly, learn and belong? Here's what you need and how to get started.

## What you'll need

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-account-check:{ .lg .middle } __A VATSIM account__

    ---

    K Global flies on the [VATSIM](https://vatsim.net) network. A free VATSIM
    account is your licence to fly online with live air traffic control.

-   :material-desktop-classic:{ .lg .middle } __A flight simulator__

    ---

    Any of the major desktop simulators works. Bring the aircraft you love —
    the fleet is broad enough that there's a place for it.

-   :material-clipboard-list:{ .lg .middle } __A VAMSYS pilot profile__

    ---

    Flights are dispatched and logged through VAMSYS. You'll set up a pilot
    profile as part of joining.

</div>

## What you get

- **Variety** — nine business units and around 421 aircraft, flagship to
  turboprop to freighter. Fly what you love.
- **A real network** — ten hubs across seven regions and roughly 848 city
  pairs, all live and ready to fly.
- **A crew room** — group flights, a welcoming community, and help whenever you
  need it. The crew is the airline.
- **Always something on** — a monthly unit spotlight plus evergreen and seasonal
  challenges to fly toward.
